Hi there.

I'm a designer currently living in Brooklyn and working in NYC.

I'm Ian Rose a UX Designer at NBC News Digital. I strongly believe that good design must start with researched and planned content. I take pride in designing and building clean, accessible, creative interactive media. I get pumped about CSS, HTML, typography, mobile development, responsive web design, and the idea that people use the things that I create. Before NBC News I worked in New Orleans for the design studio Thinka and before that the ad agency Zehnder Communications. I'm also the creator of Typesettings.
